\# ARGUS



\## Project



ARGUS is an AI Cyber Decision Intelligence Platform designed for Critical National Infrastructure.



The platform collects telemetry from endpoint agents, performs behavioural anomaly detection, predicts attack progression, maps incidents to MITRE ATT\&CK, retrieves CERT-In guidance using Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), and provides explainable remediation through an interactive dashboard.



\---



\## Vision



ARGUS is NOT another SIEM.



It is an AI Decision Intelligence Layer.



Existing monitoring systems detect.



ARGUS explains.



ARGUS predicts.



ARGUS recommends.
